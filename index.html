Story

The match head man is a detective from outer world, he came to the wasteland to investigate a murder. He finds a girl in danger at the murder scene, so he saves her, and knows that she came here to find her brother...

Character

Match head man is a mysterious detective, no one knows where is he from and what is behind his match head decoration, the only thing we know about him is that he charges a lot.
